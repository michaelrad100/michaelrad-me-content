I stepped back from Holstee operations in 2025 after 15 years. Read, traveled, thought about what was next.

This spring I came back, part-time, for a focused 8-week run. We're calling it Runway. Four 2-week sprints (Calibrate, Examine, Build, Land), May 5 through June 29.

I'm coming back as a contributor. The team has been running it. My job is to look at the operational layer and call out what's worth modernizing. Some of that is shipping new things across the content engine, support workflow, and monthly reporting. Some of it is naming what we've been doing implicitly so the next person can pick it up.

I'll write a weekly check-out here, mirroring what I share with the team in Slack. First one is up. New ones every Monday through June.
