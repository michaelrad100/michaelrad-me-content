Until this week, michaelrad.me lived on Gamma. Gamma was good for what I needed in 2024 — a one-page narrative, polished, no maintenance — but it had no surface for the work I'm doing now. No blog, no "what I'm working on," no place to drop ARC or Vibe Check beside the longer Holstee story.

So I rebuilt it. Astro for the static pages, Railway for hosting, and the blog and now-page authored as plain markdown in my Obsidian vault. The setup means I can write a post the same way I write any other note — backlinks, tasks, the whole flow — and the site picks it up on the next build.

This is the first post. More to come.
